## Channel Strategy Framework

### Channel Selection Criteria
1. **Audience Alignment**
   - Where is your audience?
   - Demographics match
   - Behavior patterns
   - Platform usage

2. **Campaign Objectives**
   - Awareness: Display, Social, Video
   - Consideration: Social, Display, Search
   - Conversion: Search, Retargeting, Shopping

3. **Budget Considerations**
   - Cost per platform
   - Minimum spend requirements
   - Expected ROI
   - Testing budget

4. **Creative Requirements**
   - Ad format capabilities
   - Production resources
   - Creative refresh needs
   - Testing capacity

5. **Measurement Capabilities**
   - Tracking implementation
   - Attribution models
   - Reporting granularity
   - Integration with analytics

## Channel Characteristics

### Search (Google Ads, Bing)
**Strengths:**
- High intent traffic
- Precise targeting
- Immediate results
- Measurable ROI

**Best For:**
- Lower funnel
- Direct response
- Lead generation
- E-commerce

**Key Metrics:**
- Quality Score
- Impression Share
- CPC, CPA
- Conversion Rate

### Display (GDN, DV360, TTD)
**Strengths:**
- Broad reach
- Visual storytelling
- Retargeting capabilities
- Brand awareness

**Best For:**
- Upper funnel
- Brand building
- Retargeting
- B2B awareness

**Key Metrics:**
- Viewability
- CTR
- View-through conversions
- Brand lift

### Social (Meta, LinkedIn, Snapchat)
**Strengths:**
- Detailed targeting
- Engagement opportunities
- Native ad formats
- Community building

**Best For:**
- All funnel stages
- Community engagement
- Brand building
- Lead generation

**Key Metrics:**
- Engagement rate
- Social actions
- Cost per engagement
- Audience growth

### Programmatic (DV360, TTD)
**Strengths:**
- Automated buying
- Real-time bidding
- Advanced targeting
- Scale and efficiency

**Best For:**
- Large-scale campaigns
- Data-driven targeting
- Cross-device reach
- Advanced attribution

**Key Metrics:**
- Win rate
- CPM
- Viewability
- Brand safety score

### Video (YouTube, Social Video)
**Strengths:**
- Engaging format
- Storytelling
- Brand building
- Viral potential

**Best For:**
- Awareness
- Consideration
- Product demos
- Brand storytelling

**Key Metrics:**
- View rate
- Completion rate
- Engagement
- Brand lift

## Multi-Channel Strategy

### Channel Mix Optimization
1. **Test and Learn**
   - Start with 2-3 channels
   - Test with meaningful budget
   - Measure incrementality
   - Scale winners

2. **Budget Allocation**
   - Based on performance
   - Consider funnel stage
   - Account for saturation
   - Reserve testing budget

3. **Cross-Channel Attribution**
   - Multi-touch attribution
   - Understand assist value
   - Avoid double-counting
   - Credit appropriately

4. **Unified Messaging**
   - Consistent brand voice
   - Coordinated campaigns
   - Sequential messaging
   - Frequency management
