## Marketing Terminology Resources

### Why Glossaries Matter

**Importance:**
- Ensure consistent terminology across teams
- Clarify ambiguous terms
- Onboard new team members faster
- Align with industry standards
- Improve communication with stakeholders

**Common Terms to Clarify:**
- **Impressions** vs. **Reach**: Impressions = total views, Reach = unique viewers
- **CTR** vs. **Engagement Rate**: CTR = clicks/impressions, Engagement = interactions/reach
- **CPA** vs. **CPL**: CPA = cost per acquisition, CPL = cost per lead
- **ROAS** vs. **ROI**: ROAS = revenue/ad spend, ROI = (revenue-cost)/cost
- **Conversion** vs. **Conversion Rate**: Conversion = action count, Rate = percentage
- **Session** vs. **User**: Session = visit, User = unique person
- **Bounce Rate** vs. **Exit Rate**: Bounce = single-page sessions, Exit = last page of session

### Glossary Usage Tips

**When to Use:**
1. **Onboarding**: New team members learning terminology
2. **Reporting**: Ensuring consistent definitions
3. **Client Communication**: Explaining metrics clearly
4. **Documentation**: Defining terms in reports
5. **Training**: Teaching marketing concepts
6. **Troubleshooting**: Clarifying misunderstandings

**Best Practices:**
- Create internal glossary based on these sources
- Link to glossaries in reports
- Use consistent terminology across all materials
- Update glossary as industry evolves
- Train team on key terms

### Key Glossary Categories

**Performance Metrics:**

**Engagement Metrics:**
