## Social Media Best Practices

### Creative Best Practices

#### Image Ads
- High-quality visuals
- Minimal text (20% rule deprecated but still good practice)
- Clear branding
- Mobile-optimized
- Test multiple variations

#### Video Ads
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Captions/subtitles (80% watch without sound)
- Vertical or square format
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- Clear CTA

#### Carousel Ads
- Tell a story across cards
- Consistent design
- Each card can stand alone
- Use all available cards
- Strong first card

### Targeting Best Practices

#### Audience Layering
- Combine demographics + interests
- Use custom audiences
- Create lookalikes from best customers
- Test audience expansion
- Exclude converters

#### Audience Sizing
- Too narrow: <50k (limited delivery)
- Sweet spot: 500k-2M
- Too broad: >5M (less relevant)
- Platform-dependent

#### Custom Audiences
- Website visitors (pixel-based)
- App users
- Customer lists (CRM)
- Engagement audiences (video, page, Instagram)
- Offline events

#### Lookalike Audiences
- Based on best customers
- 1-10% similarity (1% most similar)
- Test different percentages
- Refresh source audience regularly
- Multiple seed audiences

### Budget & Bidding

#### Budget Strategies
- **Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO)**: Let platform allocate across ad sets
- **Ad Set Budgets**: Manual control per ad set
- **Daily vs. Lifetime**: Daily for ongoing, lifetime for fixed periods

#### Bidding Strategies
- **Lowest Cost**: Maximize results within budget
- **Cost Cap**: Control average cost per result
- **Bid Cap**: Control maximum bid
- **Target Cost**: Maintain stable CPA (deprecated in Meta)
- **ROAS Goal**: Optimize for return on ad spend

### Testing & Optimization

#### A/B Testing
- Test one variable at a time
- Sufficient sample size
- Statistical significance
- Document learnings
- Iterate continuously

#### Variables to Test
- Audiences
- Creative (images, videos, copy)
- Placements
- Ad formats
- CTAs
- Landing pages

#### Optimization Frequency
- Daily: Monitor for issues
- Weekly: Analyze performance, make adjustments
- Monthly: Strategic review, budget reallocation
- Quarterly: Campaign refresh, creative updates

## Social Media Metrics & KPIs

### Engagement Metrics
- **Likes**: Basic engagement
- **Comments**: Deeper engagement
- **Shares/Retweets**: Amplification
- **Saves**: Intent to return
- **Engagement Rate**: (Engagements / Reach) Ã— 100

### Reach Metrics
- **Impressions**: Total ad views
- **Reach**: Unique users reached
- **Frequency**: Impressions / Reach
- **Share of Voice**: Brand mentions vs. competitors

### Conversion Metrics
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Clicks / Impressions
- **Conversion Rate**: Conversions / Clicks
- **Cost Per Click (CPC)**: Spend / Clicks
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Spend / Conversions
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue / Spend

### Video Metrics
- **Video Views**: 3-second views (Meta), varies by platform
- **Completion Rate**: % who watched to end
- **Average Watch Time**: Time spent watching
- **ThruPlay**: Watched 15 seconds or to end

2. **Diversify Placements**: Test all placements
3. **Use Dynamic Creative**: Let platform optimize
4. **Implement Pixel**: Proper conversion tracking
5. **Build Custom Audiences**: Retargeting foundation

1. **Target Precisely**: Use professional attributes
2. **Use Lead Gen Forms**: Native forms convert better
3. **Test Document Ads**: Unique format for thought leadership
4. **Implement Matched Audiences**: Retargeting and ABM
5. **Focus on Value**: Professional, educational content

4. **Target Young**: Focus on under-35 audience
5. **Test Story Ads**: Native format
