## 4. Implementation Checklist

1. **Define Objectives**: Align insight needs with business goals (Salesforce, Adobe, ADA guides).  
2. **Audit Data Sources**: Inventory first/second/third-party data (cross-reference DATA_SOURCES.md).  
3. **Build Activation Plan**: Map insights to campaigns, creative, budgeting (Deepsense, Digital Silk).  
4. **Establish Measurement Stack**: Use Nielsen, HubSpot stats, and Invoca/Measured to define KPIs.  
5. **Operationalize**: Use case studies (APSIS, Triggerbee) to model execution playbooks.  
6. **Optimize & Scale**: Revisit analytics loops, expand into AI agents, audience intelligence (Delve, Invoca).
