## Search Channel Comparison

### Google Ads vs. Bing Ads

| Feature | Google Ads | Bing Ads (Microsoft) |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Market Share** | ~90% search market | ~6-8% search market |
| **Audience** | Broader, younger | Older, higher income |
| **CPC** | Generally higher | 30-50% lower CPC |
| **Competition** | Very competitive | Less competitive |
| **Ad Formats** | More options | Similar core formats |
| **Device** | Mobile-heavy | Desktop-heavy |
| **Best For** | Maximum reach | Cost-efficient reach |

## Search Advertising Best Practices

### Keyword Strategy

#### Keyword Match Types
1. **Broad Match**
   - Widest reach
   - Includes variations, synonyms
   - Use with Smart Bidding
   - Monitor search terms closely

2. **Phrase Match**
   - Moderate reach and control
   - Includes close variations
   - Good balance
   - Recommended for most campaigns

3. **Exact Match**
   - Highest control
   - Exact intent match
   - Lower volume
   - Best for high-value keywords

4. **Negative Keywords**
   - Essential for all campaigns
   - Prevent irrelevant clicks
   - Regular review needed
   - Build comprehensive lists

#### Keyword Research
- Use keyword planner tools
- Analyze search term reports
- Competitor analysis
- Long-tail opportunities
- Seasonal trends

### Ad Copy Best Practices

#### Headlines
- Include keywords
- Clear value proposition
- Call-to-action
- Test multiple variations
- Use all available headlines

#### Descriptions
- Expand on value
- Include benefits
- Address pain points
- Clear CTA
- Test variations

#### Extensions
- Sitelinks (additional links)
- Callouts (key benefits)
- Structured snippets (categories)
- Call extensions (phone)
- Location extensions (address)
- Price extensions (pricing)

### Bidding Strategies

#### Manual CPC
- Full control
- Good for testing
- Requires active management
- Best for small budgets

#### Enhanced CPC (ECPC)
- Semi-automated
- Adjusts bids for conversions
- Good transition strategy
- Maintains some control

#### Target CPA
- Automated bidding
- Optimize for cost per acquisition
- Requires conversion data
- Good for lead gen

#### Target ROAS
- Automated bidding
- Optimize for return on ad spend
- Requires revenue tracking
- Best for e-commerce

#### Maximize Conversions
- Fully automated
- Spend budget to get most conversions
- Good for awareness
- Monitor CPA closely

#### Maximize Conversion Value
- Automated bidding
- Optimize for revenue
- Requires value tracking
- Best for e-commerce with varying values

### Quality Score Optimization

**Components:**
1. **Expected CTR** (40%)
   - Historical click performance
   - Ad relevance to search
   - Improve with better targeting

2. **Ad Relevance** (30%)
   - Keyword-ad alignment
   - Message match
   - Improve with tighter ad groups

3. **Landing Page Experience** (30%)
   - Page relevance
   - Load speed
   - Mobile-friendliness
   - Improve with better landing pages

**Benefits of High Quality Score:**
- Lower CPC
- Better ad positions
- Higher impression share
- Improved ROI

## Search Campaign Structure

### Account Structure
```
Account
└── Brand Campaigns
    ├── Exact Match Brand Terms
    ├── Phrase Match Brand Terms
    └── Competitor Terms
└── Non-Brand Campaigns
    ├── Product Category 1
    │   ├── High-Intent Keywords
    │   └── Informational Keywords
    ├── Product Category 2
    └── Generic Terms
└── Shopping Campaigns
    ├── High Priority (Best Sellers)
    ├── Medium Priority (Standard)
    └── Low Priority (Catch-All)
└── Dynamic Search Ads
    └── Website Coverage
```

### Ad Group Structure
- **Tight themes**: 5-20 keywords per ad group
- **Similar intent**: Group keywords by search intent
- **Consistent messaging**: Ads match keyword theme
- **Multiple ads**: 2-3 ads per ad group for testing

## Search Metrics & KPIs

### Primary Metrics
- **Impressions**: Ad visibility
- **Clicks**: User engagement
- **CTR**: Click-through rate (Clicks/Impressions)
- **CPC**: Cost per click
- **Conversions**: Goal completions
- **Conversion Rate**: Conversions/Clicks
- **CPA**: Cost per acquisition
- **ROAS**: Return on ad spend (Revenue/Cost)

### Quality Metrics
- **Quality Score**: 1-10 rating
- **Impression Share**: % of available impressions
- **Lost IS (Budget)**: Impressions lost due to budget
- **Lost IS (Rank)**: Impressions lost due to ad rank
- **Average Position**: Ad placement (deprecated in Google)

### Advanced Metrics
- **Search Impression Share**: % of search network impressions
- **Top Impression Share**: % of impressions at top of page
- **Absolute Top IS**: % of impressions in #1 position
- **Click Share**: % of available clicks received
- **Conversion Delay**: Time from click to conversion

## Search Advertising Strategies

### Brand Protection
- Bid on brand terms
- Prevent competitor conquesting
- Maintain top position

- Monitor competitor activity

### Competitor Conquesting
- Bid on competitor brands

- Expect higher CPC
- Monitor profitability
- Legal considerations

### Long-Tail Strategy
- Target specific, low-volume keywords
- Lower competition
- Higher intent
- Better conversion rates
- Use phrase/exact match

### Seasonal Campaigns
- Plan ahead for peak seasons
- Adjust bids for demand
- Create seasonal ad copy
- Expand keyword lists
- Monitor budget closely
