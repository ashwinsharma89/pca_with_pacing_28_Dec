## Display Advertising Overview

### What is Display Advertising?

**Definition**: Visual banner ads shown on websites, apps, and platforms across the internet.

**Ad Formats:**
- Banner ads (various sizes)
- Rich media ads (interactive)
- Video ads
- Native ads
- Interstitial ads
- Expandable ads

**Targeting Methods:**
- Contextual (content-based)
- Behavioral (user actions)
- Demographic
- Geographic
- Retargeting/Remarketing
- Lookalike audiences

**Key Platforms:**
- Google Display Network (GDN)
- Display & Video 360 (DV360)
- The Trade Desk (TTD)
- Amazon DSP
- Xandr (Microsoft)
- Verizon Media (Yahoo)

### Display Ad Sizes (IAB Standard)

**Desktop:**
- 728×90 (Leaderboard)
- 300×250 (Medium Rectangle)
- 160×600 (Wide Skyscraper)
- 300×600 (Half Page)
- 970×250 (Billboard)
- 970×90 (Super Leaderboard)

**Mobile:**
- 320×50 (Mobile Banner)
- 300×250 (Mobile Rectangle)
- 320×100 (Large Mobile Banner)

**Responsive:**
- Adaptive sizes (recommended)
- Automatically adjusts to available space

## Video Advertising Overview

### Video Ad Formats

**1. In-Stream Video**
- **Pre-roll**: Before content (skippable/non-skippable)
- **Mid-roll**: During content
- **Post-roll**: After content

**2. Out-Stream Video**
- **In-Feed**: Within content feed
- **In-Article**: Within article content
- **Interstitial**: Between page transitions

**3. Video Discovery**
- Thumbnail + text
- User-initiated viewing
- YouTube discovery ads

**4. Bumper Ads**
- 6-second non-skippable
- High impact, quick message

### Video Platforms
- YouTube (Google Ads)
- Facebook/Instagram Video
- TikTok
- Snapchat
- Connected TV (CTV)
- Over-the-Top (OTT)
- DV360 (programmatic video)
- The Trade Desk (programmatic video)

### Video Metrics
- **Video Views**: Platform-specific definition
- **View Rate**: Views / Impressions
- **Completion Rate**: % watched to end
- **Average Watch Time**: Time spent watching
- **Engagement**: Clicks, interactions
- **Brand Lift**: Awareness increase
- **VTR (View-Through Rate)**: Conversions after viewing

## Programmatic Advertising Deep Dive

### Programmatic Ecosystem

**Components:**
1. **DSP (Demand-Side Platform)**: Advertiser/agency buying platform
2. **SSP (Supply-Side Platform)**: Publisher selling platform
3. **Ad Exchange**: Marketplace connecting DSPs and SSPs
4. **DMP (Data Management Platform)**: Audience data management
5. **Ad Server**: Ad delivery and tracking

**Buying Methods:**
1. **Real-Time Bidding (RTB)**: Auction-based, impression-by-impression
2. **Programmatic Direct**: Automated guaranteed inventory
3. **Private Marketplace (PMP)**: Invitation-only auctions
4. **Preferred Deals**: Fixed-price, first-look inventory

### Programmatic Bidding Strategies

**1. Fixed CPM**
- Set price per thousand impressions
- Predictable costs
- Good for brand campaigns

**2. Dynamic CPM**
- Bid adjusts based on value
- Optimize for performance
- Good for direct response

**3. CPA Goal**
- Target cost per acquisition
- Algorithm optimizes bids
- Requires conversion data

**4. ROAS Goal**
- Target return on ad spend
- Revenue-based optimization
- Best for e-commerce

**5. Viewable CPM (vCPM)**
- Pay only for viewable impressions
- Reduces waste
- Industry standard

## Display & Video Best Practices

### Creative Best Practices

**Display Ads:**
- Clear, simple message
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Prominent CTA
- Brand consistency
- Multiple sizes
- Animated (but not distracting)
- File size optimization
- Mobile-optimized

**Video Ads:**
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Clear branding early
- Captions/subtitles
- Optimized for sound-off viewing
- Multiple durations (6s, 15s, 30s)
- Strong CTA
- Mobile-first design

### Targeting Best Practices

**Layered Targeting:**
- Combine multiple targeting methods
- Balance reach and precision
- Test incrementally
- Monitor performance by layer

**Frequency Management:**
- Set frequency caps
- Avoid ad fatigue
- Vary creative by frequency
- Monitor frequency impact

**Brand Safety:**
- Use brand safety tools
- Whitelist trusted sites
- Exclude sensitive categories
- Monitor placements regularly
- Use verification partners

### Measurement & Attribution

**Viewability:**
- MRC standard: 50% of pixels, 1 second (display)
- MRC standard: 50% of pixels, 2 seconds (video)
- Target: 70%+ viewability
- Optimize for viewable impressions

**Attribution:**
- View-through conversions (VTC)
- Click-through conversions (CTC)
- Multi-touch attribution
- Cross-device attribution
- Incrementality testing

**Brand Lift:**
- Brand awareness surveys
- Ad recall measurement
- Consideration lift
- Purchase intent
- Control vs. exposed groups

## Programmatic KPIs

### Performance Metrics
- **Impressions**: Ad views
- **Viewable Impressions**: MRC-compliant views
- **Clicks**: User clicks
- **CTR**: Click-through rate
- **Conversions**: Goal completions
- **Conversion Rate**: Conversions / Clicks
- **CPA**: Cost per acquisition
- **ROAS**: Return on ad spend

### Quality Metrics
- **Viewability Rate**: % viewable impressions
- **Invalid Traffic (IVT)**: % fraudulent traffic
- **Brand Safety Score**: % safe impressions
- **Completion Rate**: % video completed (video)
- **Engagement Rate**: Interactions / Impressions

### Efficiency Metrics
- **CPM**: Cost per thousand impressions
- **vCPM**: Cost per thousand viewable impressions
- **CPC**: Cost per click
- **Win Rate**: % of auctions won
- **Bid Density**: Opportunities to bid
