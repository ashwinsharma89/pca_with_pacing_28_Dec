## Google Ads Optimization

### Core Optimization Principles

#### Campaign Structure Best Practices
1. **Account Organization**
   - Separate campaigns by goal (awareness, consideration, conversion)
   - Use clear naming conventions
   - Implement proper campaign hierarchy
   - Separate branded and non-branded campaigns

2. **Ad Group Structure**
   - Keep ad groups tightly themed (5-20 keywords per group)
   - Use Single Keyword Ad Groups (SKAGs) for high-value terms
   - Separate match types into different ad groups
   - Maintain 3-5 ads per ad group for testing

3. **Keyword Strategy**
   - Start with exact and phrase match
   - Use broad match with Smart Bidding
   - Implement negative keyword lists
   - Regular search term review (weekly)
   - Maintain keyword-to-ad relevance

#### Bidding Strategies

**Manual Bidding**
- Use for: New campaigns, testing, tight budget control
- Best for: Experienced advertisers with time to manage

**Automated Bidding**
- **Target CPA**: For conversion-focused campaigns
- **Target ROAS**: For e-commerce and revenue optimization
- **Maximize Conversions**: For volume with flexible CPA
- **Maximize Conversion Value**: For revenue maximization
- **Target Impression Share**: For brand awareness

**Smart Bidding Best Practices**
- Allow 2-3 weeks learning period
- Provide sufficient conversion data (30+ conversions/month)
- Use conversion tracking properly
- Set appropriate targets based on historical data
- Monitor performance weekly, adjust monthly

#### Ad Copy Optimization

**Responsive Search Ads (RSAs)**
- Use all 15 headlines and 4 descriptions
- Include keywords in at least 2 headlines
- Pin important headlines to position 1
- Test different calls-to-action
- Use ad strength indicator (aim for "Excellent")

**Ad Extensions**
- **Sitelinks**: 4-6 per campaign
- **Callouts**: Highlight unique selling points
- **Structured Snippets**: Show product/service categories
- **Call Extensions**: For phone-based businesses
- **Location Extensions**: For local businesses
- **Price Extensions**: For e-commerce
- **Promotion Extensions**: For sales and offers

### Advanced Google Ads Techniques

#### Audience Targeting
1. **In-Market Audiences**: Target users actively researching
2. **Affinity Audiences**: Reach users based on interests
3. **Custom Intent**: Create audiences based on keywords/URLs
4. **Customer Match**: Upload email lists for targeting
5. **Similar Audiences**: Expand reach to lookalikes
6. **Remarketing**: Re-engage previous visitors

#### Performance Max Campaigns
- **Setup**: Provide high-quality assets (images, videos, headlines)
- **Asset Groups**: Create 3-5 asset groups per campaign
- **Audience Signals**: Add customer lists and intent signals
- **Budget**: Start with 3x your target CPA
- **Monitoring**: Focus on conversion value and ROAS
- **Optimization**: Refresh assets every 2-3 months

#### Search Campaign Optimization
- **Quality Score**: Aim for 7+ on all keywords
- **Ad Relevance**: Ensure keywords appear in ads
- **Landing Page Experience**: Fast load times, mobile-friendly
- **Expected CTR**: Write compelling ad copy
- **Negative Keywords**: Add 20-50 per campaign
- **Search Terms**: Review weekly, add high-performers

## Display & Video 360 (DV360) Optimization

### DV360 Overview
Display & Video 360 is Google's enterprise-level demand-side platform (DSP) for programmatic advertising, offering advanced targeting, inventory access, and optimization capabilities.

### Key DV360 Features

#### Inventory Access
- **Open Exchange**: Access to Google Ad Exchange
- **Private Marketplace (PMP)**: Negotiated deals with publishers
- **Programmatic Guaranteed**: Reserved inventory at fixed CPM
- **Preferred Deals**: First look at inventory before open auction

#### Targeting Capabilities
1. **Audience Targeting**
   - First-party data integration
   - Third-party audience segments
   - Google Audiences
   - Custom affinity and intent audiences

2. **Contextual Targeting**
   - Keywords and topics
   - Site and app categories
   - Brand safety controls
   - Viewability standards

3. **Geographic Targeting**
   - Country, region, city, postal code
   - Radius targeting
   - Location exclusions
   - Geo-fencing

4. **Device Targeting**
   - Desktop, mobile, tablet, CTV
   - Operating systems
   - Device models
   - Browser targeting

### DV360 Campaign Types

#### Display Campaigns
- **Standard Display**: Banner ads across web
- **Native**: Ads that match site design
- **Rich Media**: Interactive ad formats
- **Dynamic Creative**: Personalized ads

#### Video Campaigns
- **YouTube**: In-stream, bumper, discovery ads
- **CTV/OTT**: Connected TV advertising
- **In-Stream**: Pre-roll, mid-roll, post-roll
- **Out-Stream**: Video ads on non-video content

#### Audio Campaigns
- **Streaming Audio**: Spotify, Pandora, etc.
- **Podcast Advertising**: Targeted audio ads
- **Radio**: Digital radio advertising

### DV360 Optimization Strategies

#### Bid Strategy Optimization
1. **Fixed Bidding**: Set CPM for brand campaigns
2. **Maximize Clicks**: For traffic generation
3. **Target CPA**: For conversion campaigns
4. **Target ROAS**: For revenue optimization
5. **Viewable CPM**: Pay only for viewable impressions

#### Creative Optimization
- **A/B Testing**: Test 3-5 creative variations
- **Dynamic Creative Optimization (DCO)**: Personalized ads
- **Frequency Capping**: Limit ad exposure (3-5 per week)
- **Sequential Messaging**: Tell story across multiple ads
- **Responsive Display Ads**: Auto-optimized formats

#### Inventory Optimization
- **Brand Safety**: Use IAS or DoubleVerify
- **Viewability Targeting**: Aim for 70%+ viewability
- **Fraud Prevention**: Enable fraud detection
- **Contextual Targeting**: Align with relevant content
- **Site Lists**: Create inclusion/exclusion lists

## Cross-Platform Optimization

### Google Ads + DV360 Integration

#### When to Use Each Platform

**Use Google Ads When**:
- Budget < $50K/month
- Focus on search and shopping
- Need simple campaign management
- Want automated optimization
- Targeting Google properties primarily

**Use DV360 When**:
- Budget > $50K/month
- Need advanced targeting options
- Want access to premium inventory
- Require granular control
- Running cross-channel campaigns

#### Integration Strategies
1. **Unified Measurement**
   - Use Google Analytics 4 for both
   - Implement cross-platform attribution
   - Create unified dashboards
   - Track user journeys across platforms

2. **Audience Sharing**
   - Share remarketing lists
   - Use Customer Match across both
   - Sync conversion data
   - Create lookalike audiences

3. **Budget Optimization**
   - Allocate based on performance
   - Use DV360 for awareness, Google Ads for conversion
   - Test channels in Google Ads, scale in DV360
   - Monitor cross-platform ROAS

### Performance Monitoring

#### Key Metrics by Platform

**Google Ads Metrics**:
- CTR (Click-Through Rate)
- Quality Score
- Conversion Rate
- CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
- Impression Share
- Search Lost IS (Budget/Rank)

**DV360 Metrics**:
- Viewability Rate
- Completion Rate (video)
- Brand Lift
- Reach and Frequency
- Cost Per Completed View (CPCV)
- Active View CPM
- Post-View/Post-Click Conversions

#### Reporting Best Practices
1. **Daily Monitoring**
   - Budget pacing
   - Conversion tracking
   - Major anomalies

2. **Weekly Reviews**
   - Performance trends
   - Search term reports
   - Audience performance
   - Creative performance

3. **Monthly Analysis**
   - Goal achievement
   - Budget allocation
   - Strategic adjustments
   - Competitive analysis

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Google Ads Issues

**Low Quality Score**
- Solution: Improve ad relevance, landing page experience
- Add keywords to ad copy
- Improve landing page load time
- Ensure mobile-friendliness

**High CPA**
- Solution: Refine targeting, improve ad copy
- Add negative keywords
- Test different bid strategies
- Improve landing page conversion rate

**Low Impression Share**
- Solution: Increase bids or budget
- Improve Quality Score
- Expand keyword list
- Adjust targeting

### DV360 Issues

**Low Viewability**
- Solution: Adjust inventory targeting
- Use viewability targeting
- Exclude low-viewability sites
- Adjust ad sizes

**High CPM**
- Solution: Expand targeting
- Use open exchange
- Adjust bid strategy
- Negotiate PMPs

**Low Reach**
- Solution: Expand audience targeting
- Increase frequency cap
- Add more inventory sources
- Adjust geographic targeting
